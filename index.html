The 3-Style method is an advanced technique for solving the 3×3 Rubik’s Cube blindfolded (3BLD). It involves using three distinct algorithms or “styles” to efficiently solve the corners, edges, and orientations of the cube. This method requires a strong foundation in blindfolded solving and familiarity with advanced cube notation. [Source]
